About the concert

What help can be provided?

How do volunteers help people with disabilities? They can help with the organization of leisure and recreation, engage in art therapy with their ward, teach something, talk about something, read a book, teach how to use a modern telephone. Volunteers can help with cleaning, caring for bedridden patients, or just sit with a person with a disability while his relatives decide some of their own business. Volunteers can take a dog for a walk, help someone in a wheelchair down the stairs… There are so many different, most simple things in which the help of a caring person is invaluable!

What are the benefits of volunteers?

Our company will financially provide all those who help, and give certificates for the portfolio. This will help volunteers in building their future, getting into a good university, or finding a prestigious job with a well-paid salary.
